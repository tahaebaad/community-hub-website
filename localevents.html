Charity Run

Date: December 9, 2024

Location: Community Park

Description: Join us for an invigorating charity run aimed at raising funds for local education initiatives. The event will feature a 5k, 10k, and kids’ fun run, making it suitable for participants of all ages and skill levels. Enjoy a morning of exercise, live music, and a post-run celebration with food trucks and community booths. All proceeds from the event will go toward providing school supplies, scholarships, and extracurricular programs for underprivileged students in our area.

Art Festival

Date: December 12, 2024

Location: Downtown Art Center

Description: Experience a vibrant celebration of creativity at the Downtown Art Center. This year’s art festival features an array of local artists showcasing their work in various media, including painting, sculpture, photography, and mixed media. Interactive workshops, live demonstrations, and a special kids’ art zone ensure there’s something for everyone. Don’t miss the evening gala, where you can meet the artists and enjoy live performances while supporting the arts in our community. Food vendors and a cozy lounge area make it the perfect day out for art enthusiasts and families alike.

Food Festival

Date: December 15, 2024

Location: Riverside Pavilion

Description: Savor culinary delights from around the world at our annual Food Festival. With over 30 food stalls featuring cuisines from local chefs and international vendors, this event is a paradise for food lovers. Enjoy live cooking demonstrations, contests, and musical performances while indulging in mouthwatering dishes. A dedicated kids’ play area ensures fun for the whole family!

Winter Wonderland

Date: December 20, 2024

Location: City Square

Description: Celebrate the holiday season at our Winter Wonderland event. Enjoy ice skating, a Christmas market, and live performances from local choirs and bands. Don’t miss the tree lighting ceremony and the special visit from Santa Claus. Warm up with hot cocoa and festive treats while creating memories with your loved ones in a magical winter setting.
